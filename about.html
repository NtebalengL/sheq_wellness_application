About SHEQ+

SHEQ+ is a free platform built to support children and women across South Africa with safety resources, anonymous reporting and up-to-date guidance.

Why we built SHEQ+

Teenage pregnancy and gender-based violence remain critical issues in South Africa. Contributing factors include poverty, limited access to sexual education, social norms, and insufficient local support services. SHEQ+ aims to reduce harm by providing an accessible place to report, learn, and get directed to help.

What we do

  • Allow anonymous incident reporting that notifies administrators
  • Provide AI-powered, up-to-date guidance on safety and health
  • Offer a directory of helplines and local resources

Sources included in our FAQ section are taken from reputable organisations such as UNICEF, WHO, and South African government reports.
